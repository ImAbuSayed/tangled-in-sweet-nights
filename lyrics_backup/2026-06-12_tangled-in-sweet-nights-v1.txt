[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Midnight falling soft across the wooden floor
I never knew I'd want a single moment more
Every single glance is burning up the room
Tangled up in shadows underneath the moon

[Verse 1]
Pour a glass of something heavy, let it breathe
The quiet of the evening pulling at my sleeve
I can feel the gravity in how you move
Falling into rhythm, tracing out the groove

[Pre-Chorus]
There is something hiding in the way you smile
Telling me to linger for a little while
Every heavy heartbeat echoes down the hall
I am only waiting for the final call

[Chorus]
Wrapped up in the magic of a sweet night
Drowning in the feeling, holding on tight
Got me spinning helpless in your warm gaze
Lost inside a beautiful and slow maze

[Post-Chorus]
(Holding on tight, yeah we're holding on tight)
We're tangled in the magic of a sweet night

[Verse 2]

Feeding all the embers of a wild desire
Shadows playing softly on your painted lips
Slipping out of focus at my fingertips

[Bridge]
The hands of time are freezing in the gentle dark
Every touch you leave is leaving such a mark
I am caught between the longing and the quiet space
Even Saeed gets lost inside your endless grace
There is nothing left to carry, nothing left to hide
Just a rising river pulling from the inside

[Spoken]
(Just let the morning wait a little longer)
(You are the only truth I want to know)

Wrapped up in the magic of a sweet night
